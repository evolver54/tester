AIR MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Fire at him right after he finishes blowing out a full wave of tornadoes. He always pauses briefly before starting the next pattern.

  • Manage the Tornado Walls: His tornadoes form layered “walls” that move toward you. Look for the gaps — there is always at least one safe lane you can slip through without jumping wildly.

  • The Positioning Trick: Stay mid‑range. Too close and you get hit by the tornadoes before you can react; too far and the gaps become harder to align with. Mid‑range gives you the best visibility.

  • Weakness: The Leaf Shield (from Wood Man) deals heavy damage and can hit him even through some of his tornado patterns. It’s one of the fastest ways to bring him down.
  • Pattern: Air Man cycles between blowing out tornado walls and using his fan to push you back. The tornado patterns vary slightly, but the rhythm of “tornado → tornado → tornado → wind push” is consistent.

  • Start with Air Man: Many players tackle him early because his weapon, the Air Shooter, is extremely effective against Crash Man and useful for hitting enemies above you.

  • Jump Strategy: Use short, controlled hops to slip through the tornado gaps. Big jumps make you drift into the next tornado layer or get pushed by his fan.

  • Stage Tips: The floating platforms with the giant robot heads are the main challenge. Time your jumps carefully and don’t rush — the heads fire in predictable bursts, so wait for the safe moment before moving.
