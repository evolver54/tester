CRASH MAN

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Shoot him right after he lands from a jump. He pauses for a split second before launching his Crash Bombs, giving you a clean opening.

  • Manage the Vertical Movement: Crash Man jumps constantly and tracks your position. Keep your own jumps controlled so you don’t drift into his bombs or lose track of where he’ll land.

  • The Positioning Trick: Stay on the ground as much as possible. Crash Man tends to jump toward you, and grounded movement gives you the best chance to sidestep his landings.

  • Weakness: The Air Shooter deals heavy damage and hits him easily during his jumps. It’s one of the fastest ways to bring him down.
  • Pattern: He alternates between jumping toward you and firing Crash Bombs. His movement is predictable, but the bombs stick to surfaces and explode after a delay, so don’t linger near them.

  • Start with Crash Man: Many players fight him mid‑game. His weapon, the Crash Bomber, is essential for breaking walls and is required to reach certain Wily stage areas.

  • Jump Strategy: Use short hops only when necessary to avoid his bombs. Big jumps make you an easy target since he tracks your vertical position aggressively.

  • Stage Tips: The long ladder sections are the main challenge. Watch out for the flying enemies that try to knock you off — take them out early or wait for safe openings before climbing.
