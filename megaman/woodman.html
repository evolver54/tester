WOOD MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Fire at him right after he drops his Leaf Shield. He always pauses for a moment before launching his leaf volley, giving you a clean opening.

  • Manage the Leaf Pattern: His falling leaves come down in a predictable wave. Stay calm and weave between them rather than trying to outrun them.

  • The Positioning Trick: Stand at mid‑range. Too close and the Leaf Shield hits instantly; too far and the falling leaves become harder to dodge. Mid‑range gives you the best reaction time.

  • Weakness: The Atomic Fire (Heat Man’s weapon) can destroy him in just a few charged shots. Even uncharged, it deals strong damage.
  • Pattern: He cycles between activating Leaf Shield, throwing it, then sending down a rain of leaves. The rhythm is slow and consistent once you learn it.

  • Start with Wood Man: Many players fight him mid‑game. His weapon, the Leaf Shield, is great for clearing small enemies and is especially effective against Air Man.

  • Jump Strategy: Jump straight up to avoid the falling leaves rather than jumping forward. Vertical jumps help you stay aligned with the safe gaps.

  • Stage Tips: Watch out for the robotic wolves that breathe fire — they take several hits. The gorilla robots in the forest section are easier if you stay mobile and shoot while backing up.
