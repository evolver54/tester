Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Shoot him right after he finishes his flame dash. He always pauses for a moment before starting the next charge, giving you a reliable opening.

  • Manage the Flame Bursts: His three‑shot fire pattern spreads outward in predictable arcs. Stay calm and slip between the gaps instead of jumping too early.

  • The Positioning Trick: Stay slightly off‑center. Being directly in front of him makes his dash harder to dodge, but standing too far away gives him more room to build speed.

  • Weakness: The Bubble Lead deals heavy damage and interrupts some of his attacks. It’s one of the safest ways to chip him down without risking close‑range hits.
  • Pattern: Heat Man cycles between firing three flame bursts and launching a high‑speed dash. The dash always tracks your position at the moment he ignites, so keep moving.

  • Start with Heat Man: Many players fight him mid‑game. His weapon, the Atomic Fire, is powerful and can be charged to deal massive damage to several other Robot Masters.

  • Jump Strategy: Jump only to avoid the flame arcs — never to dodge the dash. His dash is too fast to jump over consistently, so sidestepping is safer.

  • Stage Tips: The disappearing block section is the main challenge. Memorize the rhythm or use the Item‑2 platform to skip the entire sequence if you have it.
